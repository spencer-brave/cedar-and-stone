How It Works

From idea to printed book

We've refined this process across dozens of books. Every step is designed to make it easy for you and exceptional for your audience.

1

Discovery

We start with a conversation about your vision, your audience, and the kind of book that feels authentically you. No templates. Every project starts from scratch.

2

Story Development

Our writers work with you to shape the narrative, building a story that captivates kids, resonates with parents, and carries your voice throughout. Child development advisors weigh in on pacing, themes, and age-appropriate engagement.

3

Illustration & Design

We match you with an illustrator whose style fits your vision. You'll see sketches, give feedback, and watch the art come to life. until every page is something you're proud to put your name on.

4

Production

We handle printing, materials, binding, and quality control. Premium production at scale. The kind of book that feels good in your hands and survives hundreds of bedtimes.

5

Launch

This is where your audience meets the book. We build the entire launch strategy: pre-orders, bundles, email campaigns, landing pages, fulfillment, all coordinated around your content calendar to maximize impact.
